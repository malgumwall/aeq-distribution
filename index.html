FAQ

Questions, answered

What kind of inventory do you sell?

Brand-name closeout, overstock, and shelf-pull inventory across grocery, beverage, candy, HBA, cleaning, electronics, toys, and general merchandise. New loads are sourced weekly.

What are your minimums?

We work from a single pallet up to full truckloads. There's no minimum just to start a conversation — message us and we'll match loads to your volume.

How is pricing set?

Loads are priced well below standard wholesale — often up to 70% below retail — so there's real margin left for you. You get unit counts and per-unit cost up front.

How fast can I get a load?

Quotes typically come back within 24 hours, and orders ship in about 3–5 business days depending on volume and destination.

Do you ship to exporters?

Yes. We regularly supply exporters with consistent, documentable loads ready for container freight.

How do I order?

Everything runs through WhatsApp. Message us your category, target volume, and price — we'll send current loads with photos and lock in your order.
