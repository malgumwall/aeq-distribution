FAQ

Common questions

What kind of inventory do you sell?

Brand-name grocery and beverage first, plus closeout, overstock, and shelf-pull inventory across candy, HBA, cleaning, and everyday merchandise. We supply brick-and-mortar retail, wholesale, and export.

What are your minimums?

We work from a single pallet up to full truckloads. There's no minimum to start a conversation, so message us and we'll match loads to your volume.

How is pricing set?

Loads are priced below standard wholesale so there's real margin left for you. You get unit counts and per-unit cost up front, before you commit.

Do you ship to exporters?

Yes. We can supply exporters with consistent, documentable loads ready for container freight.

How do I order?

Everything runs through WhatsApp. Send us your category, target volume, and price, and we'll send current loads with photos and lock in your order.
